CashParking is an online domain monetization system that quickly and easily lets you earn money from your existing domain portfolio. Each time someone clicks on advertising that generates revenue on your parked pages, you share in the revenue. CashParking offers the most competitive revenue sharing in the industry and is backed by the world-class customer support you've come to expect from Go Daddy.
Pick the CashParking revenue share plan that fits your budget and return expectations.
Add your domains to your CashParking portfolio using our simple bulk add tool. You can have an unlimited number of domains in your CashParking account.
Change your existing name servers on your domains to the Go Daddy parkweb servers for CashParking.
We will then present ads on your domain's parked page and generate revenue reports so you can follow your progress.
GET PAID according to the revenue share you purchased, using the payment method you select. Domestic customers will receive payment monthly, International customers will receive payment quarterly.
