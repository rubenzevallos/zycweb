WordPress offers an open-source, state-of-the-art publishing platform that focuses on aesthetics, Web standards and usability. The largest self-hosted blogging tool in the world, WordPress is used on hundreds of thousands of sites and seen by tens of millions of people every day.

If you’re wondering how to install WordPress and use it as your blogging tool, you first need a Web host. Web Hosting is the business of housing, serving and maintaining Web site files. Go Daddy’s world-class Website hosting offers auto-install of WordPress, plus services that include email, databases, free applications, FREE access to the exclusive Hosting Community, and much more. Unlike many of its competitors, Go Daddy owns, operates and supports its hosting services.

To choose the right hosting plan, you’ll need to consider a few things: How was your Web site designed? How much traffic or how many visitors do you expect monthly? How large is your site?

If you host a traffic-intensive Web site, manage multiple Web sites or install specific server software, consider a Go Daddy dedicated or virtual dedicated server. Anticipate changing Web hosting demands? Then consider Grid Hosting.

Grid Hosting is a flexible hosting platform that matches performance with demand. If your site's traffic spikes, Grid Hosting responds by allocating more resources and keeping your site live. If your site starts small, but is growing, Grid Hosting grows with it so you don't have to manually upgrade and experience any unnecessary downtime. Grid Hosting "pools" the resources of many servers and your site's content resides on multiple servers. This networked system helps achieve a high reliability—beyond 99.9%—for your Web site because if one server shuts down, only a fraction of the grid's resources are lost.

So there you have it – with WordPress and WordPress Hosting from Go Daddy you’ll have a completely customizable tool that can be used for almost anything, from a family home page to a business Web site. You can even include the WordPress Photo Gallery to share photos with family, friends and business associates.
