EFT/ACH INSTRUCTIONS

An Electronic Funds Transfer or Automated Clearing House (EFT/ACH) transfers money directly between your bank and ours to establish or increase your Good As Gold balance.

Most banks allow you to create an EFT/ACH through their website. Your bank’s customer representatives can also help you find the specific details for creating an EFT/ACH.

To establish or increase your Good As Gold balance:
Use information from the "Transfer To:" form below to set up the transaction with your bank. Once the transfer is complete, we'll notify you that your balance has been increased. At that point, you're ready to use Good as Gold for purchases.
