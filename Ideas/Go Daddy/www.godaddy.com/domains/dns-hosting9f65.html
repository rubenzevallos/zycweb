DNS security is vital. There's a reason why there are DNS hosting companies, DNS providers and managed DNS services all across the Internet. Without managed DNS, you're gambling on the availability, security and performance of your site. Premium DNS puts you in control of your website's DNS so you can make sure your visitors and customers reach your site consistently and safely.

So what does managed DNS do for you? First, it makes your website more reliable by eliminating 404 (website not found) errors. Secondly, it improves the performance of your website. By having your DNS records on multiple servers across the United States, Europe and Asia, using the global Anycast network, visitors from around the world are able to access your site faster because there are more DNS servers near them. Lastly, Premium DNS offers DNS security that prevents hackers from re-routing your visitors to malicious websites, intercepting their personal data, launch DDoS (Denial of Service) attacks and more.

Premium DNS is different than most DNS providers and managed DNS services. It offers the same rock-solid DNS security you demand from managed DNS, but with easy-to-use features and tools that you won't find with other DNS hosting – and all for a price that can't be beat.

While most DNS providers or DNS hosting companies only allow you to manage a small number of domains, Premium DNS gives you unlimited domains, so your entire catalog is covered. The same is true with records – while other managed DNS services max out at 1,000, Premium DNS gives you unlimited records.

In fact, Premium DNS is the only managed DNS to include a complete collection of features like Secondary DNS, bulk updates, vanity name server, advanced reporting and more, all for free! Best of all, unlike every other DNS security company, there are no overage fees with Premium DNS.
