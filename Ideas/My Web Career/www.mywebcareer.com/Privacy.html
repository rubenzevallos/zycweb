Information we collect and/or retrieve

Certain areas of our site do not require you to register and thus are available to you without you providing any personal information. In order to leverage full product or service functionality offered through our website, we may collect the following type of information:

  • Information you provide – when you signup for our service, our signup process utilizes personal information such as your name, email address, and zip code if available. Personal information may also include information you supply to us concerning your preferences and interests expressed in the course of use of our site.

  • Profile information about you retrieved from third party(s) – in order to benefit from the full functionality of the service, you must allow MyWebCareer access to your personal, social, and business profile data such as your LinkedIn profile or Facebook profile. In such instances, you’re authorizing MyWebCareer access to your profile data using OAuth. Simply put, OAuth allows you to authorize us to retrieve data on your behalf without giving us your credentials to these third party sites.

  • Publicly available information that may be about you retrieved through Internet Search engines – MyWebCareer is a data aggregator, and as such we’ll seed searches against Internet search engines using your personal information to retrieve data that may or may not related to you. This retrieved information along with your personal and profile information may be used to build your career, personal and social footprints.

  • Logs - When you visit our website, our servers automatically record information that your browser sends. These logs may include, but is not limited to, information such as your web request, Internet Protocol address, browser type, the page you were visiting prior to our site, the date and time of your request, and one or more cookies.

  • Cookies - MyWebCareer utilizes cookies to provide a personalized product experience and for track usage. We may use “session ID cookies” to enable certain features of the site, to better understand how you interact with our site, and to estimate audience size and monitor aggregate usage patterns. You can configure your browser to accept, reject, or prompt you to accept cookies before you visit websites. However, some MyWebCareer features and services may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

Access to your personal information is strictly restricted and used in accordance with specific internal procedures and safeguards governing access, in order to operate, develop or improve our service.
