Explore your Network

Visually explore your merged LinkedIn and Facebook network.

MyWebCareer provides a straight-forward and easy to use visual interface for exploring your merged Careeer & Social Graph. Your Network Chart positions you in the center, just click on nodes to expand and explore your relationships.

You get visual cues to help you determine if the realtionship to the company or person is derived from your LinkedIn or Facebook network, or both. An easy to use filter window enables you to browse through people, company and university connections and click to focus your chart on these entities.
