Career Score

Your Career Score is a numerical representation of the strength of your Professional Online Brand. Think of it as a Credit Score but based on your online rather than financial behavior.

Your Career Score is generated based on a number of factors:

  • References we find on the Internet to the the positions you've held

  • Your LinkedIn Profile

  • References we find on the Internet searching for your name only

  • Your Social Network Presence (Facebook and Twitter)

MyWebCareer Premium provides a detailed breakdown of all factors influencing your Career Score and guidance on how to improve your score.
